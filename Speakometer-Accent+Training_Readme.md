Key Components of the Process
Input & Selection: Users select their target accent (British or American) and listen to a native speaker audio sample.
Recording: The app uses the microphone to record the user's attempt at the word or phrase.
AI Analysis: An Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) engine compares the user’s recording to native pronunciation models.
Feedback Mechanism: The app provides immediate feedback, including verbal, visual, and color-coded analysis (e.g., green for correct, red/yellow for incorrect).
IPA & Correction: The app uses International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) guides to help users understand specific sound errors.
Tracking: User progress is monitored via performance scores and progress charts.
